My Journey

I began my professional path in the healthcare sector, where I worked closely with people, developed strong soft skills, and learned to be adaptable, responsible, and detail-oriented.

After several years in that field, I decided to pursue a more creative and technical direction — web development. I enrolled in a professional certificate program in Web Application Development (CP3), where I gained hands-on experience with HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, PHP, MySQL, and Git.

I’m currently focused on building accessible and responsive websites, and I’m looking for my first opportunity to contribute to real-world tech projects as a junior developer.

Outside of work and studies, I'm passionate about hands-on technology. I enjoy 3D printing, building DIY electronics, and working with microcontrollers and development boards. I also explore radio-based technologies, which help me stay curious, solve practical challenges, and apply structured thinking beyond the screen.

These hobbies fuel my creativity, reinforce my logical mindset, and complement my approach to coding and continuous learning.
