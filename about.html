My Journey

In recent years, I decided to transition into a more technical and creative field — web development. I completed a professional program in Web Application Development (CP3), gaining hands-on experience with HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, PHP, MySQL and Git, and later expanding my skills into WordPress site building and customization.

Today, I focus on building accessible, responsive and efficient websites, and I’m actively looking for my first opportunity as a Junior Web Developer to contribute to real-world projects and continue growing professionally.

Outside of coding, I enjoy hands-on technology such as 3D printing, DIY electronics and microcontrollers. I also explore radio-based technologies, which help me stay curious, solve practical challenges, and bring a structured mindset to my work as a developer.

My goal is to join a team where I can grow as a developer, contribute to meaningful projects, and keep improving every day.
